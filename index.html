Nike, Inc. is a multinational corporation based in Beaverton, Oregon, specializing in the design, manufacturing, marketing, and sale of athletic footwear, apparel, equipment, and accessories. Established in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports and rebranded to Nike in 1971, the company is recognized for its innovative sports products and cutting-edge designs. Its iconic “Swoosh” logo and “Just Do It” slogan have made it of the most recognizable and valuable brands in the world. Nike collaborates with top athletes and sports teams, and its product range spans professional and casual use, emphasizing performance and style.
